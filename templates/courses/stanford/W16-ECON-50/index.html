Textbooks

The required readings will be drawn from Besanko and Braeutigam, Microeconomics, 5e (abbreviated B&B). Available at the bookstore, on Amazon, and on scribd. Note that this book is at a more basic level than the level of the course; it is necessary but not sufficient to understand the material at the level of this book.

If you're serious about econ, it may be helpful to pick up a copy of Varian, Intermediate Microeconomics: A Modern Approach, 8e. Available on reserve at the library or at Amazon. Note that this book is at a slightly more advanced level than the level of the course, and the terminology may not match up exactly with what we're doing in class.
